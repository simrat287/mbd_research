About Our Research

Our aim is to understand and combat mosquito-borne diseases, which pose a significant threat to global health. We focus on developing innovative solutions to prevent, diagnose, and treat these diseases.

Our Research

What We've Done

  • Developed a novel mosquito trap that reduces mosquito populations by 90%
  • Identified a new genetic marker for dengue fever susceptibility
  • Created a low-cost, rapid diagnostic test for Zika virus

What We Hope to Do

  • Develop a vaccine against malaria
  • Investigate the role of environmental factors in mosquito-borne disease transmission
  • Create a mobile app for disease surveillance and tracking
